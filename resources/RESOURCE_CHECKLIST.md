# AutoBat Resource and Requirement Checklist

Place collected material in the matching folder under `resources/`. Do not put
customer records, production credentials, or unredacted personal data in Git.

## Brand Assets

Target folder: `brand/`

- Primary AutoBat logo in SVG or AI/EPS
- Transparent PNG logo at high resolution
- Monochrome and inverse logo variants
- Brand color values
- Official fonts and font licenses
- Brand guidelines
- App icon and favicon, if they exist
- Approved product and factory photography
- Dealer/distributor signage examples

## Product Data

Target folder: `products/`

- Current product catalog
- SKU/model master spreadsheet
- Product family and application mapping
- Voltage, capacity, dimensions, and technical specifications
- Vehicle make/model/fuel-to-battery fitment chart
- Product pack shots
- Current QR/barcode/serial label samples
- Discontinued and replacement-model mapping

## Warranty Material

Target folder: `warranty/`

- Warranty cards for every product family
- Warranty policy and terms
- Free-replacement and pro-rata tables
- Effective dates for policy revisions
- Late-registration and missing-invoice rules
- Replacement warranty rules
- Claim inspection checklist
- Rejection reason list
- Existing claim, job card, and approval forms
- Anonymized examples of approved and rejected claims

## Dealer and Distributor Operations

Target folder: `operations/`

- Dealer and distributor onboarding forms
- Territory hierarchy
- Current order, invoice, challan, and receipt formats
- Stock transfer and return forms
- Existing dealer/distributor spreadsheets
- Credit, scheme, and incentive rules for later phases
- Sales, warranty, dispatch, and claim standard operating procedures

## Delivery and Recycling

Target folder: `delivery/`

- Delivery challan and proof-of-delivery formats
- Vehicle and driver master fields
- Damage, shortage, and rejection processes
- Old-battery exchange/rebate rules
- Pickup, weight, and recycler documents
- Compliance reports currently submitted

## Technical and Integration

Target folder: `integrations/`

- ERP/accounting software name and owner
- API documentation or export samples
- Existing serial-number source
- WhatsApp Business provider details
- SMS provider details
- Email domain/provider details
- Maps and routing preferences
- Current hosting/security policies
- Data retention and backup requirements

## People and Approval Matrix

Target folder: `discovery/`

- Project sponsor
- Product owner
- Warranty process owner
- Sales and distribution owner
- Dispatch/delivery owner
- Finance/ERP owner
- IT/security owner
- Dealer and distributor pilot participants
- Approval levels for date corrections, policy overrides, claims, and replacements

## Required Workshops

1. Management goals and success metrics
2. Product/serial master and factory dispatch
3. Distributor/dealer stock movement
4. Retail sale and warranty activation
5. Claim inspection and settlement
6. Delivery, returns, and old-battery collection
7. ERP, reporting, security, and rollout
